Black Sesame Ice Cream

Prep Time

30m

Cook Time

15m

Chilling Time

8h

Total Time

8h 45m

Ingredients

  • 2Tbsp Toasted Black Sesame Seeds
  • 400ml Whole Milk
  • 70g Sugar
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2Tbsp Honey
  • 3Tbsp Black Sesame Paste
  • 1Tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 200ml Whipping Cream
  • A Pinch of Salt

Method

  1. Measure and prepare all ingredients
  2. Grind the toasted black sesame seeds and put aside
  3. Whisk Sugar and egg yolk together until it becomes pale yellow
  4. Once it turns pale yellow add in: honey, black sesame seeds (ground), black sesame paste and whisk until well combined
  5. Simmer the milk and then add to the mixture in a slow stream. Mix at a low speed for 30 seconds
  6. Transfer mixture into a small pot
  7. Heat the mixture over medium-low heat and stirring constantly until it thickens and reaches 80oC
  8. Remove from heat and cool down the pot in a large bowl filled with iced water. Then Add vanilla while it cools down
  9. Add whipped cream and a pinch of salt into the cooled sesame mixture
  10. Fold in but do not over mix it. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for minimum 8hours (overnight).
  11. After completely cooled, process the mixture in your ice cream makeer for about 25mins. The mixture will slowly thicken/freezes and become ice cream
  12. After it freezes in the ice cream maker, transfer it to a container and then it is ready to serve
