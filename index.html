Black Sesame Ice Cream

Prep Time

30m

Cook Time

15m

Chilling Time

8h

Total Time

8h 45m

Ingredients

  • 2Tbsp Toasted Black Sesame Seeds
  • 400ml Whole Milk
  • 70g Sugar
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2Tbsp Honey
  • 3Tbsp Black Sesame Paste
  • 1Tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 200ml Whipping Cream
  • A Pinch of Salt

Method

Step 1

Measure and prepare all ingredients.

Step 2

Grind the toasted black sesame seeds and put aside.

Step 3

Whisk the 70g Sugar and egg yolk together until it becomes pale yellow.

Step 4

Once it turn pale yellow add in: honey, black sesame seeds (ground), black sesame paste and whisk until well combined.

Step 5

Simmer the milk and then add to the mixture in a slow stream. Mix at a low speed for 30 seconds.

Step 6

Transfer mixture into a small pot. Heat the mixture over a medium-low heat and stirring constantly until it thickens and reaches 80oC.

Step 7

Remove from heat and cool down the pot in a large bowl filled with iced water. Then Add vanilla while it cools down.

Step 8

Add the whipped cream and a pinch of salt into the cooled sesame mixture. Then start fold in to mix but do not go overboard. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for minimum 8hours (overnight).

Step 9

After completely cooled, process the mixture in your ice cream makeer for about 25mins. The mixture will slowly thicken/freeze and become ice cream.

After it freezes in the ice cream maker, transfer it to a container and then it is ready to serve.
